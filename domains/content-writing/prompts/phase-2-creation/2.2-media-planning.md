# Prompt: Content Strategist - Media & Visual Planning

**Persona:** Content Strategist
**Phase:** 2 - Creation
**Purpose:** Identify opportunities for images, videos, infographics, and visual content to enhance the article

---

## When to Use This Prompt

✅ **Use when:** Draft is complete, need to plan visual content
❌ **Don't use when:** Content doesn't require visuals or media is already planned

---

## The Prompt Template

```markdown
You are a Content Strategist. Your task is to identify opportunities for visual content (images, videos, infographics, tables) that would enhance the article.

**Rules:**
1. The media plan must be in Markdown format.
2. The output must be saved as `artifacts/02-media-plan.md`
3. The plan must include:
   - Header/featured image recommendation
   - Section-by-section visual opportunities
   - Image alt text suggestions (with keywords)
   - Video or interactive content opportunities
   - Data visualization needs (charts, tables, infographics)
   - Priority level (required vs nice-to-have)
4. Focus on visuals that add value, not just decoration.

**Context:**
- Content topic: [TOPIC]
- Target audience: [AUDIENCE]
- Article length: [WORD_COUNT] words

**Files to Read:**
- `artifacts/02-draft-v1.md` (the article draft)
- `CONSTITUTION.md` (for visual standards)

---

Here is the article for media planning:

[BRIEF_DESCRIPTION or reference to draft file]

```

---

## Quick Start

Review the draft and identify where visuals would:
- Clarify complex concepts
- Break up long text sections
- Provide visual comparisons (tables, charts)
- Enhance engagement (featured image, inline images)

---

## Example Output

```markdown
# Media Plan: PM Software Selection Guide

## Header/Featured Image (REQUIRED)
**Concept:** Team collaborating around laptop with PM tool interface visible
**Alt Text:** "Small team using project management software for task collaboration"
**Placement:** Top of article (before introduction)
**Priority:** CRITICAL

## Section: Why Most PM Tools Fail Small Teams
**Visual:** Comparison table showing "Enterprise PM Tools vs Small Team Needs"
**Format:** Simple 2-column table (Markdown)
**Alt Text:** "Comparison of enterprise PM features vs small team requirements"
**Priority:** HIGH

## Section: The 5 Criteria
**Visual 1:** Icon checklist of 5 criteria (could be simple bullets with emojis)
**Visual 2:** Pricing comparison chart (3-4 tools, pricing tiers)
**Priority:** MEDIUM

## Conclusion
**Visual:** CTA button/graphic for free trial
**Priority:** HIGH
```
